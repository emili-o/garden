Nov 20, 2024

Methadone-dispensing machines represent a technological intervention in medication-assisted therapy, costing clinics between $7-10k per unit. Medetomidine

Nov 18, 2024

Medetomidine is a veterinary anesthetic drug that has been found in samples of street drugs in the US since 2022. Like xylazine, medetomidine is a potent sedative that is not approved for use in humans.

Also like xylazine, it is an alpha 2-adrenergic agonist which is thought to mimic the longer-lasting sedation associated with actual heroin.

The inclusion of this drug and xylazine in the dope supply has complicated overdose response. These drugs cause heavy sedation and increase the amount of respiratory depression that individuals experience. Since they’re not opioids,1 they don’t respond to naloxone.

The drugs in Kensington are cheap and plentiful

Nov 18, 2024

In Kensington, dope is so cheap and the customer base so reliable, that dealers often give the stuff away for free. When this happens, word travels quickly, and it’s not an uncommon sight to see 20-30 people suddenly rushing in the same direction.

When you do have to pay for it, you’re usually looking at $5 for a bag. Sometimes they’ll have bags for a dollar, though this isn’t the norm, and you can assume that a $1 bag of any drug isn’t going to be the purest thing on the market.
